# Platform-Specific Launch Guides

## Platform Selection by Product Type

### Developer Tools
**Priority:** GitHub, Hacker News, Reddit, X, Dev.to

### B2B SaaS
**Priority:** LinkedIn, Product Hunt, G2/Capterra

### B2C/Consumer
**Priority:** X, TikTok, Instagram, YouTube

### Indie Hacker
**Priority:** Product Hunt, Indie Hackers, X, Reddit

---

## Product Hunt

### Profile Requirements
- [ ] Maker profile complete (bio, photo, social links)
- [ ] Company/product page created
- [ ] At least 5 previous upvotes on other products
- [ ] Verified email

### Launch Assets
- **Thumbnail:** 240x240px, simple, recognizable
- **Gallery:** 4-6 images at 1270x760px
- **Tagline:** Max 60 characters, benefit-focused
- **Description:** Clear problem/solution, features, CTA
- **First Comment:** Your story, why you built it, ask for feedback
- **Video:** Optional but recommended (1-2 min demo)

### Supporter Strategy

**Target: 500+ commitments before launch**

1. Export LinkedIn connections (1st degree)
2. Export email contacts
3. List community members (Discord, Slack)
4. Identify Product Hunt friends

**DM Template:**
```
Hey [Name],

I'm launching [Product] on Product Hunt next [Day].

[One sentence about what it does and why it matters]

Would love your support on launch day - just need 30 seconds
to upvote and check it out.

Can I count you in?
```

**Follow-up (2 days before):**
```
Quick reminder - launching [Product] tomorrow!

Here's the direct link: [URL]

Thanks again for your support!
```

### Launch Day Checklist
- [ ] Launch at 12:01 AM PT
- [ ] First comment posted immediately
- [ ] Social posts scheduled throughout day
- [ ] Team ready to respond to comments
- [ ] Track ranking throughout day
- [ ] Thank supporters publicly

## LinkedIn

### Profile Optimization
- [ ] Professional headshot
- [ ] Compelling headline (not just job title)
- [ ] About section tells your story
- [ ] Featured section with product links
- [ ] Experience includes current company

### Content Strategy

**Best Performing Formats:**
1. Personal journey/story posts
2. Lessons learned (numbered lists)
3. Behind-the-scenes building content
4. Customer success stories
5. Industry insights/hot takes

**Posting Cadence:**
- 3-5 posts per week for launch period
- Post between 8-10 AM local time
- Engage with comments within first hour

**Writing Tips:**
- Hook in first line
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences)
- Use line breaks for readability
- End with question or CTA
- No links in post body (put in comments)

### Engagement Tactics
- [ ] Join relevant groups
- [ ] Comment on influencer posts
- [ ] Tag relevant connections
- [ ] Respond to every comment

## X (Twitter)

### Profile Setup
- [ ] Clear profile photo
- [ ] Cover image with product/brand
- [ ] Bio: What you do + building [Product]
- [ ] Pinned tweet about product
- [ ] Website link

### Content Mix
- 40% Building in public (progress, struggles)
- 30% Industry insights/value
- 20% Product updates/features
- 10% Direct promotion

### Thread Strategy

**Launch Announcement Thread:**
```
1. Hook: What you launched and why it matters
2. Problem: The pain point you're solving
3. Solution: How your product solves it
4. Features: 3-4 key capabilities
5. Social proof: Early results/testimonials
6. CTA: Link to try it
7. Ask: Retweet request
```

### Hashtag Strategy
- Use 1-2 relevant hashtags max
- #buildinpublic for journey posts
- Industry-specific tags for reach
- Don't overdo it (looks spammy)

## Reddit

### Community Selection
Find subreddits where your audience hangs out:
- r/SaaS
- r/startups
- r/Entrepreneur
- r/[YourIndustry]
- r/SideProject

### Engagement Rules

**DO:**
- Be a community member first
- Provide value without promoting
- Share genuinely helpful content
- Respond thoughtfully to others

**DON'T:**
- Drop links without context
- Post promotional content only
- Ignore subreddit rules
- Use multiple accounts

### Launch Post Format
```
Title: I built [Product] to solve [Problem] - looking for feedback

Body:
- Brief intro (who you are)
- Problem you noticed
- What you built
- What makes it different
- Ask for specific feedback
- Link (if allowed by sub rules)
```

## Hacker News

### Show HN Guidelines
- Only post products you made
- Be ready for technical questions
- Accept criticism gracefully
- Engage actively in comments

### Title Format
```
Show HN: [Product Name] – [One-line description]
```

**Good:** `Show HN: Acme – Open source analytics for developers`
**Bad:** `Show HN: We built an AMAZING analytics platform!!!`

### Best Posting Times
- US work hours (9 AM - 5 PM ET)
- Avoid weekends for launches
- Tuesday-Thursday typically best

### Comment Strategy
- Respond to every question
- Be technical and specific
- Share architecture/stack details
- Accept feedback genuinely

## Indie Hackers

### Profile Setup
- [ ] Connect revenue tracking
- [ ] List all products
- [ ] Complete milestone timeline
- [ ] Active in groups

### Content Types
1. **Milestone posts:** Revenue updates, user milestones
2. **Questions:** Ask for genuine feedback
3. **Interviews:** Request and give interviews
4. **Group posts:** Engage in relevant groups

### Community Norms
- Transparency about numbers
- Support other founders
- Share failures, not just wins
- Long-term relationship building

## G2 & Capterra (B2B)

### Listing Requirements
- [ ] Complete company profile
- [ ] Product description with features
- [ ] Pricing information
- [ ] Screenshots and demo video
- [ ] Integration list

### Review Strategy
- Ask happy customers directly
- Make review process easy (direct link)
- Respond to all reviews (good and bad)
- Never fake reviews

### Optimization
- Use relevant category keywords
- Update listing regularly
- Monitor competitor listings
- Respond quickly to inquiries
