# Example Deck Plans

## Example 1: SaaS Product Pitch

### DECK-PLAN.md

```markdown
# DataFlow Product Pitch

## Deck Overview
- **Audience:** Series A investors
- **Goal:** Secure $2M funding
- **Key Message:** DataFlow is the fastest-growing data automation platform for mid-market companies
- **Slides:** 12

## Slide Plan

### Slide 1: Title
- **Type:** Title
- **Headline:** DataFlow
- **Subhead:** Automated data pipelines for growing teams
- **Visual:** Abstract data flow animation
- **Key Message:** Modern, technical, trustworthy

### Slide 2: Problem
- **Type:** Problem Statement
- **Headline:** Data engineering is broken for mid-market teams
- **Content Points:**
  - 73% of data projects fail to deliver on time
  - Average team spends 20 hours/week on manual data tasks
  - Hiring data engineers costs $150K+/year
- **Visual:** Frustrated team illustration + statistics
- **Key Message:** This is a massive, expensive problem

### Slide 3: Solution
- **Type:** Solution
- **Headline:** DataFlow automates your data pipeline in minutes
- **Content Points:**
  - No-code interface
  - 200+ pre-built connectors
  - Automatic error handling
- **Visual:** Product screenshot showing drag-and-drop interface
- **Key Message:** Easy, powerful, ready to use

### Slide 4: How It Works
- **Type:** Process
- **Headline:** From raw data to insights in 3 steps
- **Content Points:**
  1. Connect your sources (5 minutes)
  2. Transform with visual builder (no SQL)
  3. Sync to your warehouse automatically
- **Visual:** 3-step horizontal flowchart
- **Key Message:** Simple process, powerful results

### Slide 5: Traction
- **Type:** Metrics
- **Headline:** 400% growth in 12 months
- **Content Points:**
  - $1.2M ARR
  - 150+ customers
  - 98% retention rate
  - 4.8/5 G2 rating
- **Visual:** Large numbers + simple growth chart
- **Key Message:** Proven demand, happy customers

### Slide 6: Market
- **Type:** Market Opportunity
- **Headline:** $12B addressable market
- **Content Points:**
  - Mid-market data automation underserved
  - 50,000 potential customers
  - Growing 25% YoY
- **Visual:** Market size chart + target customer segments
- **Key Message:** Large, growing market

### Slide 7: Business Model
- **Type:** Pricing/Revenue
- **Headline:** Simple usage-based pricing
- **Content Points:**
  - Starter: $99/month
  - Growth: $499/month (most popular)
  - Enterprise: Custom
  - 85% gross margins
- **Visual:** Pricing cards + margin breakdown
- **Key Message:** Healthy unit economics

### Slide 8: Competition
- **Type:** Competitive Advantage
- **Headline:** Built for mid-market, not enterprise
- **Content Points:**
  - Fivetran: Too expensive ($10K+ minimum)
  - Airbyte: Too technical (requires engineers)
  - Stitch: Limited connectors (100 vs our 200+)
- **Visual:** 2x2 positioning map
- **Key Message:** Clear differentiation

### Slide 9: Team
- **Type:** Team
- **Headline:** Built by data infrastructure veterans
- **Content Points:**
  - CEO: Ex-Stripe data engineering lead
  - CTO: Ex-Snowflake architect
  - 15 team members, hiring 10 more
- **Visual:** Team photos + brief bios
- **Key Message:** Experienced team, executing well

### Slide 10: Roadmap
- **Type:** Timeline
- **Headline:** The next 18 months
- **Content Points:**
  - Q1: 50 new connectors
  - Q2: Real-time sync
  - Q3: AI-powered transformations
  - Q4: Enterprise security features
- **Visual:** Horizontal timeline
- **Key Message:** Clear vision, rapid innovation

### Slide 11: The Ask
- **Type:** Investment
- **Headline:** Raising $2M Series A
- **Content Points:**
  - Use of funds: 50% engineering, 30% sales, 20% ops
  - Target: 500 customers in 18 months
  - Path to $5M ARR
- **Visual:** Fund allocation pie chart
- **Key Message:** Clear plan, achievable goals

### Slide 12: Closing
- **Type:** CTA
- **Headline:** Join us in building the future of data automation
- **Content Points:**
  - Contact: founders@dataflow.io
  - Deck: dataflow.io/investors
  - Demo: dataflow.io/demo
- **Visual:** Logo + contact info
- **Key Message:** Let's talk
```

## Example 2: Sales Proposal

```markdown
# Acme Corp Proposal: Workflow Automation

## Deck Overview
- **Audience:** Acme Corp operations team
- **Goal:** Win $50K annual contract
- **Key Message:** Our solution will save Acme 30 hours/week and pay for itself in 2 months
- **Slides:** 10

## Slide Plan

### Slide 1: Title
- **Type:** Title
- **Headline:** Workflow Automation Proposal
- **Subhead:** Acme Corp × AutoFlow
- **Visual:** Professional title with both logos
- **Key Message:** Partnership, not vendor relationship

### Slide 2: Understanding Your Challenge
- **Type:** Problem (Customized)
- **Headline:** Current state: Manual processes costing time and money
- **Content Points:**
  - Order processing: 15 hours/week manual data entry
  - Reporting: 8 hours/week consolidating spreadsheets
  - Approvals: 5 hours/week chasing sign-offs
  - Total: 28 hours/week of manual work
- **Visual:** Process flow showing pain points (based on discovery call)
- **Key Message:** We understand your specific challenges

### Slide 3: Our Solution
- **Type:** Solution
- **Headline:** AutoFlow eliminates manual work across your operations
- **Content Points:**
  - Automated order processing
  - Real-time dashboard reporting
  - Digital approval workflows
  - Integration with your existing tools
- **Visual:** Before/after comparison
- **Key Message:** Comprehensive solution, not point fixes

### Slide 4: Implementation Plan
- **Type:** Process
- **Headline:** Live in 30 days with minimal disruption
- **Content Points:**
  - Week 1: Setup and integration
  - Week 2: Configuration and testing
  - Week 3: Training and rollout
  - Week 4: Optimization and handoff
- **Visual:** 4-week timeline with milestones
- **Key Message:** Fast, professional implementation

### Slide 5: ROI Analysis
- **Type:** Metrics
- **Headline:** $75K annual savings, 2-month payback
- **Content Points:**

- **Key Message:** Risk-free, fully supported

### Slide 8: Investment

  - Platform: $4,000/month

- **Key Message:** Affordable with massive return
```
